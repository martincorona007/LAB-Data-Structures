POINTERS TO FUNCTIONS
you can create pointers to functions
to get it, it is necessary to mention the prototype of the function we want to point as shown below.
Note the use of parentheses to associate the asterisk with the name of the declaration, with them we managed to prevent
our declaration being interpreted as the declaration of a function that returns a pointer to type;

	<type> (*<name>)(<arguments>)

¡(si quieres declarar un apuntador a funcion, se necesita poner parentecis en el apuntador)!
